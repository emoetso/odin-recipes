Beef Stew

Description

Nigerian beef stew is a rich and hearty dish made with tender beef, tomatoes, peppers, and a blend of spices. It's known for its bold, savory flavors and is typically enjoyed with rice, yams, or other staples

Ingredients

  • Beef
  • tomatoes
  • scotch bonnet
  • onions
  • Curry
  • Thyme
  • Seasoning cube

Steps:

  1. Boil the beef with spices and seasoning of choice, fry or grill and set aside
  2. Blend the peppers, mine was about 4 cups after blending
  3. Using the same oil used in frying the beef, add the pepper mix and cook for some few minutes
  4. Add spices, salt and bouillon powder/ seasoning cubes and fried beef to the stew.
  5. Continue to cook on low heat till oil floats to the top of the stew (if the stew is too thick, add stock or water to make it lighter)
  6. Serve as desired
